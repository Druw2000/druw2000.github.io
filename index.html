Modular Websites

Modular website building is by far the best paradigm for quick development. When done correctly, all pages can look the same yet still be allowed to be edited independently if creative ideas want to be explored.

Building modularly should follow the KISS principle (Keep It Simple Stupid).

We do not walk around with <head>s in our <body>s, atleast most of us don't. Knowing this, we should always logically separate information, it just werks.
