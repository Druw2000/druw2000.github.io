Welcome to Realpolitik! A website

Realpolitik is the body of pragmatic implementations of ideological and moral concerns, thesepolitik. By this definition realpolitik is useless without a theoretical background.

Realpolitik is not authoritarian.

Some people may claim realpolitik is a system based soley practicality and is deaf to the concerns of ethics, civics, etc. Do not listen to these people, and disregard everything they say. 'Real' Realpolitik is anything but deaf to important considerations of ethics, civics, etc. because these important considerations are goals loudly proclaimed by important rulers.
